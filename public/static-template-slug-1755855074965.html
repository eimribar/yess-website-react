FAQ

What reporting capabilities does your Salesforce AI Agent have?

It builds any dashboard or report on command—"Weekly pipeline velocity by rep," "Account health scores by segment," or "Lead conversion funnel analysis." Posts summaries to Slack with actionable insights.

How does data hygiene work?

Your Salesforce AI Agent runs deduplication algorithms, validates email formats, standardizes company names, fills missing fields from external sources, and flags data quality issues—all under your approval workflows.

What scoring and routing can it handle?

Lead scoring based on firmographics and behavior, opportunity risk scoring using historical patterns, account prioritization for CSMs, and intelligent territory routing based on your custom rules.

Can it actually write to Salesforce data?

Yes—within objects/fields you allow. Policy-as-code rules like "only merge duplicates with confidence ≥ 95%" and "require approval for account risk score changes" with full before/after diffs.

How does it ensure data quality?

Your Salesforce AI Agent validates against your business rules, checks external data sources for enrichment, runs confidence scoring on all changes, and maintains immutable audit logs of all data operations

What's included on day one?

Pipeline hygiene dashboards, duplicate detection workflows, lead scoring models, account risk assessment, missing field enrichment, and executive reporting templates—all ready to deploy.

Built for GTM workflows

Your Salesforce AI Agent handles prebuilt skills for pipeline hygiene, account research, sequences, renewals, and exec reporting—all executed under your guardrails.
