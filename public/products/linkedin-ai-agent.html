FAQ

How does this work with Outreach/Salesloft?

LinkedIn becomes native platform functionality. Add LinkedIn steps to any sequence just like email steps. Complete data flows back automatically.

Is this safe for our LinkedIn accounts?

100% safe. Advanced behavioral mimicry, local IP rotation, human-like timing patterns. We've processed 10M+ LinkedIn actions with zero account issues.

What data flows back to Salesforce?

Everything. Connection status, messages sent/received, profile views, InMail opens. All logged as activities without any manual work.

Can it work with existing sequences?

Yes. Add LinkedIn steps to any sequence—new or existing. Mix LinkedIn connections, messages, and profile views with email touchpoints.

How does personalization work?

AI analyzes recent posts, company updates, and mutual connections to craft messages that feel personal, not automated.

What's the setup process?

30 minutes. OAuth connection within your platform. No IT tickets, no security reviews needed. Most teams see results same day.
