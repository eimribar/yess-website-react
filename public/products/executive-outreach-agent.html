FAQ

How does this work with Outreach/Salesloft?

Native integration. Executive touchpoints become sequence steps. SDRs add them to any campaign, executives approve in Slack, everything stays in your existing workflow.

Is this secure for our executives?

100% secure. OAuth connections only. No passwords stored. No LinkedIn credentials shared. No email access granted. SOC 2 compliant. Your security team will approve.

Does it really log to Salesforce automatically?

Every single touch. LinkedIn messages, emails, responses—all logged as activities in Salesforce without any manual work. Full visibility, zero effort.

How much effort for executives?

5 seconds per approval in Slack. They batch review multiple requests, see prospect context, approve or edit. SDRs handle all the targeting and timing.

What about multi-channel coordination?

Your agent handles LinkedIn and email outreach from executives, maintaining consistent messaging across channels while each touchpoint fits naturally into your sequences.

What's the setup process?

30 minutes. Connect Outreach/Salesloft, Salesforce, and Slack through OAuth. No passwords, no IT tickets. Start generating pipeline today.
