General Questions

What is Yess?

Yess is an agentic operating system for enterprise sales teams. Our platform gives every account executive an 'Agentic AE'—an AI partner that embeds the craft and process of a top 1% seller to automate complex sales execution, such as multi-threading and relationship orchestration. The result is a scalable system that turns every seller into a high-performer.

What is an Agentic AE and how is it different from other sales AI?

An Agentic AE is an AI partner that moves beyond passive analysis and into active execution. While other tools suggest what to do, an Agentic AE drafts the emails, prepares the plays, and orchestrates the stakeholders required to win a deal. It's an active executor that handles the manual work, with a human-in-the-loop for final approval, not a passive analyzer that creates more tasks.

Who is Yess for?

Yess is designed for B2B enterprise sales organizations. It provides the most value to: Sales Leaders (CROs, VPs) who need to close the performance gap and build a predictable revenue engine. Sales Managers who need to scale their coaching and enforce their winning methodology. Account Executives who need to master complex deals and automate manual execution.

How does Yess improve sales performance?

Yess improves sales performance by systematizing elite execution. Our platform closes the gap between top and average performers by embedding a winning methodology into an agentic AI. This leads to three key outcomes: 1. Increased win rates by mastering deal complexity. 2. Reduced ramp time for new hires. 3. The ability to scale what works across the entire sales floor.

What are the core features of the Yess platform?

The Yess operating system is built on four core agentic workflows: 1. Agentic Relationship Selling to orchestrate multi-threading. 2. Executable Mutual Action Plans to maintain deal momentum. 3. Proactive Risk Detection to surface unseen threats. 4. An AI Twin that serves as the rep's co-pilot for the entire deal.

Which tools does Yess integrate with?

Yess integrates seamlessly into your existing GTM stack to act as its execution layer. Key integrations include Salesforce, Slack, Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar), Microsoft 365 (Outlook), Outreach, Salesloft, and Gong. Our platform turns passive data from these tools into proactive, agentic action without disrupting your team's workflow.

How does the 'human-in-the-loop' system work?

Our 'human-in-the-loop' model ensures your team is always in control. The Agentic AE performs the preparation and execution work—like drafting an outreach email or a follow-up—and delivers it as an actionable suggestion in Slack. The user simply reviews the action and approves it with a single click. The AI does the work; the human makes the strategic decision.

Is Yess secure for enterprise companies?

Yes, Yess is built with enterprise-grade security as a foundational principle. We are SOC 2 compliant, use OAuth-only connections (no passwords stored), honor all Salesforce permissions and security rules, and ensure that your data and your customers' data are protected at every step. Your security team will approve.

Salesforce AI Agent

What reporting capabilities does the Salesforce AI Agent have?

It builds any dashboard or report on command—'Weekly pipeline velocity by rep,' 'Account health scores by segment,' or 'Lead conversion funnel analysis.' Posts summaries to Slack with actionable insights.

How does data hygiene work with the Salesforce AI Agent?

Your Salesforce AI Agent runs deduplication algorithms, validates email formats, standardizes company names, fills missing fields from external sources, and flags data quality issues—all under your approval workflows.

What scoring and routing can the Salesforce AI Agent handle?

Lead scoring based on firmographics and behavior, opportunity risk scoring using historical patterns, account prioritization for CSMs, and intelligent territory routing based on your custom rules.

Can the Salesforce AI Agent actually write to Salesforce data?

Yes—within objects/fields you allow. Policy-as-code rules like 'only merge duplicates with confidence ≥ 95%' and 'require approval for account risk score changes' with full before/after diffs.

How does the Salesforce AI Agent ensure data quality?

Your Salesforce AI Agent validates against your business rules, checks external data sources for enrichment, runs confidence scoring on all changes, and maintains immutable audit logs of all data operations.

What's included on day one with the Salesforce AI Agent?

Pipeline hygiene dashboards, duplicate detection workflows, lead scoring models, account risk assessment, missing field enrichment, and executive reporting templates—all ready to deploy.

Lead Recommendation Agent

What exactly is the Lead Recommendation AI agent?

Two AI systems working together: ICP Finder discovers new prospects matching your ideal customer profile, while Already Connected finds qualified leads from your team's existing LinkedIn network. Both deliver recommendations in Slack.

How fast is setup for the Lead Recommendation Agent?

Setup is handled by your account manager and takes under 30 minutes. Your agents start delivering qualified leads the same day.

How do the two lead generation agents work differently?

ICP Finder creates connection requests for new prospects. Already Connected skips connection requests since you're already connected and creates direct messages for warm outreach.

Can we control what types of leads we get?

Yes. Your account manager configures both agents based on your ideal customer profile, deal size, industry, and other criteria.

What happens after I approve a lead recommendation?

For ICP Finder leads: connection requests are sent with follow-up sequences prepared. For Already Connected leads: direct messages are sent to the team member with the relationship for approval.

What results should we expect from the Lead Recommendation Agent?

Teams typically see 50% more qualified prospects in their pipeline, 3x higher response rates on warm connections, and discover prospects they wouldn't find manually.
